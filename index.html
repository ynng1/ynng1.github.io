Childhood Obstructive Sleep Apnea Syndrome: Understanding the Impact on Your Child's Health, Sleep & Future

Important Health Warning
Sleep apnea can seriously affect your child's development and well-being. Early detection is crucial.

Impact of Late Diagnosis

  • Permanent growth impairment
  • Long-term academic struggles
  • Persistent behavioral issues
  • Increased risk of hypertension

Warning Signs to Watch For

  • Chronic mouth breathing
  • Frequent sore throat/choking
  • Daytime drowsiness
  • Poor school performance
  • Poor appetite

Diagnosis Process

  • Paediatric sleep specialist evaluation
  • Overnight sleep study (polysomnography)
  • Continuous monitoring of sleep and breathing
  • Non-painful procedure with caregiver present

Treatment Options

  • Surgery for enlarged tonsils/adenoids
  • Weight management for obesity cases
  • Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) therapy
  • Regular specialist follow-up
Source: SingHealth - Obstructive Sleep Apnoea in Children
